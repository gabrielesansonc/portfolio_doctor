How to get your Robinhood CSV

Based on Robinhood’s help center, use an Account activity report CSV rather than a monthly statement PDF.

  1. Open Robinhood and go to Account.
  2. Open Reports and statements.
  3. Choose Account activity report or Reports.
  4. Select Generate new report.
  5. Choose your account type and date range, then generate the report.
  6. Wait for Robinhood to finish preparing it, then download the CSV from Reports.

Robinhood says most reports are ready within a couple of hours, but they can take up to 24 hours. Futures, crypto, and spending activity may not be included in the investing account activity report.

Source: Robinhood Help Center, “Finding your reports and statements”.
